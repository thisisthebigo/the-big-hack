About The Big Hack

Welcome to The Big Hack - Acharya Institute of Technology's premier technology hackathon where brilliant minds converge to transform ideas into reality. This intensive coding marathon brings together students, developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts from across disciplines to collaborate, innovate, and build solutions that matter.

Collaborative Innovation

Work in diverse teams to combine different perspectives and expertise

Cutting-Edge Challenges

Solve problems spanning AI, sustainability, fintech, healthcare, and emerging technologies

Learn & Grow

Workshops, tech talks, and hands-on sessions throughout the event

Network & Connect

Build lasting relationships with fellow innovators and industry professionals

Win Big

Compete for substantial prizes, internship opportunities, and startup funding

Open to All

Students from any background and any college across India can participate
